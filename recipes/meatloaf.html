Meatloaf

Description

This classic meatloaf is just like grandma used to make

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • 1 Egg
  • Worshchestishire Sauce
  • Breadcrumbs

Instructions

  1. Saute the Onion
  2. Combine all the ingredients
  3. Mix well (using hands is recommended)
  4. Bake at 400 for 60 minutes. Let cool for 15 minutes before sering
