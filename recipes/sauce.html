Tomato Sauce

Description

This is a traditional Italian Style tomato sauce.

This is a second paragraph to prove I remember how to do that.

Ingredients

  • Can of Whole Peeled Tomatoes
  • Fresh Basil
  • Garlic
    • You need Olive Oil too
  • Salt and Pepper (to taste)

Instructions

  1. Sautee the Garlic
  2. Blend Tomatoes while Garlic is sauteeing
  3. Add Blended Tomatoes to Garlic
  4. Add Basil, Salt, and Pepper
  5. Cook on low heat for at least 60 minutes, stirring periodically.
